# FAQ — rpi-plan-to-speckit-plan

### What if the RPI plan is very detailed?
Keep the detail in RPI, but summarize cleanly for spec-kit communication. Preserve the important design decisions and trade-offs.

### Should I include code-level implementation detail?
No. Keep the output architectural: structure, patterns, data model, trade-offs, and non-functional constraints.

### What if my org has no formal Constitution gates yet?
Leave the Constitution Check as a template placeholder and document the gates that would logically apply.

### Can I re-run the conversion if the plan changes?
Yes. The conversion should be deterministic.

### Do I still need `rpi-review` after this?
Optional. You can review inside either workflow depending on team preference.
